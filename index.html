About Nocturnal

Nocturnal was founded upon the basis of issues plaguing the community around its founders. All too common in professional, academic, and even social contexts is a seeming culture of sleep deprivation. People are often seen either complaining about the work constraints keeping them up, or boasting about their late night out. In competitive environments, sleep is seemingly a marginal scribble in the grand scheme of things.

Technology

Neural EEG

Uses DEAP to compare with individuals homeostatic state and recognize deviations. Provisionally patented Electrosoothe technology then provides treatment therapies.

Optical Heart Monitor

Uses Photoplethysmography (PPG) to measure heart rate. Provides aromatherapy based on heart rate irregularities.

Pressure Sensors

Quantify each individual'ss movement patterns, providing data on restlessness to the mobile application.

Temperature Monitoring

Tracks user temperature in order to notify user of irregularities and provides temperature feedback for soothing purposes.

Comfort

Nocturnal takes a multifaceted approach to incorporate cutting-edge technology into a pillow without sacrificing even the slightest bit of comfort.

Layered System

Interwoven, embedded electronics in order to maximize both functionality and comfort, as naturally comfort takes priority when looking at a pillow's practicality.

Adjustability

Utilizing cross sections of hyper-pliable materials like polyurethane foams, gels, and memory foams, the nocturnal pillow seemingly allows the user to fall into a supportive cloud custom.

Our Approach

Track, Analyze, Stabilize

Track

Nocturnal uses advanced biometric sensors to take a variety of crucial inputs that allow for long term homeostatic benchmarks, irregularity indications, and more.

Analyze

Using MARS/LARS regression algorithms and other processing methods, track deviations in system activity, and provide suggestions for stabilization.

Stabilize

Given the issues or suggestions given by the analysis, take resultant action in terms of treatment and return the system to homeostatic equilibrium.
